Accept instant payments for high-risk businesses with our WooCommerce payment gateway. Receive instant payouts in USDC (Polygon) using fiat-to-crypto onramp providers.

== Description ==

AwesomePayments.net provides a powerful and seamless payment gateway for WooCommerce, allowing businesses in high-risk industries to accept payments instantly. Our plugin supports multiple payment methods, including credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Maestro), Apple Pay, Google Pay, SEPA, and local bank transfers. Payouts are processed instantly in USDC on the Polygon network, eliminating chargebacks and delays.

== Key Features ==

* Instant Access & Approval – No waiting, start accepting payments immediately.
* Multiple Payment Methods – Accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Google Pay, Apple Pay, SEPA, and more.
* No Merchant Sign-Up Required – ZERO KYB for complete anonymity.
* Designed for High-Risk Businesses – Can be installed on any website.
* Instant Crypto Payouts – Get paid in USDC (Polygon) with every order.
* No Chargebacks – Transactions are final and irreversible.
* Privacy-Focused – Each order generates a temporary wallet for enhanced security.
* Automatic Order Processing – Orders are marked as paid instantly upon confirmation.
* Admin Payout Tracking – Monitor payouts directly from the WooCommerce admin panel.
* Multi-Currency Support – Accept payments in different currencies.
* Customizable Payment Gateway Icons – Personalize payment method display.

The minimum transaction amount varies per provider. Check the [list of minimum order values per provider](https://awesomepayments.to/#faqs).

This plugin and its associated services are subject to the [Terms of Service](https://awesomepayments.net/tos/) and [Privacy Policy](https://awesomepayments.net/privacy-policy/).

== Installation ==

1. Install and activate the plugin.
2. Navigate to **WooCommerce > Settings > Payments > AwesomePayments.net Payment Gateway**.
3. Enable the preferred payment provider and enter your USDC (Polygon) wallet address for payouts.
4. Customize the payment method’s display label and description.
5. Save settings and start accepting payments instantly!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need to sign up as a merchant? =
No. The plugin works instantly without requiring merchant registration, as it relies on fiat-to-crypto onramp providers.

= When will I receive my payouts? =
Payouts are processed instantly with each order and sent directly to your USDC wallet.

= How do I fix the "No Payment Methods Available" error? =
Follow our guide on [resolving WooCommerce payment method errors](https://awesomepayments.net/woocommerce/#fix-no-payments).

= I have a problem with an order. What should I do? =
Contact the [AwesomePayments.net support team](https://awesomepayments.net/contact/) for assistance.

= I’m receiving payments, but my orders remain pending. How do I fix this? =
Our plugin is designed to mark orders as paid automatically. If issues arise, check our [troubleshooting guide](https://awesomepayments.net/woocommerce/#troubleshoot).

= Are there any restricted business categories? =
While anyone can use our payment plugin, businesses violating our [Terms of Service](https://awesomepayments.net/tos) may be blocked.
